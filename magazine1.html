TheMay issue of National Geographic Traveller Indiais travel with a focus on On the Road experiences. These are drives to remember and are filled with self-discovery and unusual encounters. They feature a tour through koala-filled forests and limestone cliffs of Australia’s Great Ocean Road. A road trip across America uses grandma’s journal as guide and a Himalayan drive with young companions opens up new vistas for a road trip junkie. A hair-raising journey takes readers around Yorkshire’s gothic haunts. Venture into the Zulu kingdom with Durban, South Africa’s city of coastal cool. The issue also packs in a short break from Bhopal at Ujjain, the city of palaces and poetry. .
